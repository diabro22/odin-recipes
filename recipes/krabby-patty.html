Krabby Patty

Description

Presenting... the Krabby Patty, a rare delicacy from the depths of Bikini Bottom.

Although the secret formula is unknown to this day, this recipe provides a close replica of the infamous culinary spectacle.

Ingredients

  • Bun
  • Patty
  • Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Pickles
  • Tomato
  • Bun

Steps

  1. Put bottom bun on plate
  2. Put tomato on top of bottom bun
  3. Put pickles on top of tomato
  4. Put lettuce on top of pickles
  5. Put cheese on top of lettuce
  6. Put patty on top of cheese
  7. Put top bun on top of patty
