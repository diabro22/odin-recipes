Green Eggs and Ham

Description

He may have been called Dr. Seuss, but the man was a culinary genius.

This delicious rendition of the popular breakfast dish of scrambled eggs and Canadian bacon will have you begging for more.

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Ham
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Crack eggs in bowl and whisk until yolk is liquidy
  2. Slice ham into thin cuts and place on oiled pan over medium-high heat
  3. Use a magical spell on eggs to turn them green
  4. And that's it! Enjoy
